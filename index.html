FAQs

Addressing the exact objections that stop participation.

Is this really free? +

Yes. The service is fully funded by the Municipal Corporation of Gandhinagar. You never pay the pickup staff.

What if I miss a pickup day? +

No problem. Your streak pauses, not resets. You can continue from the next successful pickup.

Do I need to store waste for long? +

Pickup is twice a week, so storage stays minimal. A single bag in your balcony/utility area is enough.

Where does the waste go? +

Collected dry waste is sent to authorized sorting and recycling partners. The Corporation audits the process.
